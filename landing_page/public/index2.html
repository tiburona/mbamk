My Brain and Me

Your neuroscience and psychology portal

Welcome! We are a group of Columbia University neuroscientists passionate about accelerating scientific research and helping everyone learn from psychology and neuroscience discoveries. We want to help you to use science to improve your life, and connect you to opportunities to help the research effort. Our flagship service, now in development, is a tool to help you upload a brain scan and interact with a beautiful 3D image of your own brain. If you don’t have a brain scan, we’ll help you look for a research study you can participate in to get one for free.

We have a lot of ideas about where to go from here, and we want to know what’s important to you, so we’re opening the floor. Tell us what you think we should work on next, and if you like, sign up to get updates on our projects.
