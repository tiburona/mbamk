Welcome! We are a group of Columbia University neuroscientists passionate about accelerating scientific research and helping everyone learn from psychology and neuroscience discoveries and use them to improve their lives in practical ways. Our flagship project, now in development, is a tool to help you visualize your own brain. If you don’t have a brain scan, we’ll direct you to resources that can help you find a research study in your area and get one for free.

We have a lot of ideas about where to go from here, and we want to know what’s important to you, so we’re opening the floor. Click below to subscribe for updates and tell us what you would like to see.
