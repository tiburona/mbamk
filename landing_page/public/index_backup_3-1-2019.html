Do you have an interest in brain science and wonder how it applies to you? Would you like to see inside your own brain, or learn more about your psychology? Have you participated in neuroimaging research study or had a clinical brain and always wanted to do more with your brain scan data?

We are developing a platform that enables people to learn about their brain and their psychology for recreation, education and self-improvement. Our core mission is to integrate brain science with self-exploration to accelerate discovery in neuroscience and mental health research. We also want to inspire people to make a difference by participating in research studies, online or in person.

Affiliated with New York State Psychiatric Institute, Columbia University Vagelos College of Physicians and Surgeons, and NYC MediaLab
